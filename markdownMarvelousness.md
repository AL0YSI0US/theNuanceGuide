- `bgColor` → **Background Color**
- `borderColor` → **Border Color**
- `qColor` → **Question Color**
- `aColor` → **Answer Color**
- `textColor` → **Normal text Color**
- `codeColor` → **Code Color**
- `theme` → **Theme (Must be one of the following)**
